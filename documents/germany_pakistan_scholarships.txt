DAAD SCHOLARSHIPS FOR PAKISTANI STUDENTS - GERMANY

SOURCE: DAAD Official Website (daad.de)

MONTHLY SCHOLARSHIP AMOUNTS (as of February 2026):
- Masters students: 992 EUR per month
- Doctoral candidates: 1,400 EUR per month
- Professors: 2,000-2,150 EUR per month

WHAT DAAD SCHOLARSHIP COVERS:
- Monthly stipend (992 EUR for Masters)
- Health insurance (private insurance paid by DAAD)
- Travel allowance (lump sum)
- Partner allowance: 276 EUR per month (if applicable)
- Child allowance: 259 EUR per month per child

IMPORTANT - WHAT DAAD DOES NOT COVER:
- Tuition fees (you pay these yourself)
- Baden-Wurttemberg charges 1,500 EUR per semester for non-EU students
- TU Munich charges 4,000-6,000 EUR per semester for Masters

ELIGIBILITY FOR PAKISTANI STUDENTS:
- No fixed age limit for most programmes
- Can apply if you finished degree recently
- Can apply if currently in last year of study
- Must have been in Germany less than 15 months if already there
- Must apply from Pakistan (country of origin)

LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS FOR DAAD:
- German programmes: TestDaF, DSH, Goethe-Zertifikat (level C1 often required)
- English programmes: IELTS, TOEFL iBT, Cambridge English, PTE Academic
- Language certificate must not be older than 3 years

GRADE REQUIREMENTS:
- No minimum GPA specified
- High academic achievement strongly preferred
- Other factors: motivation, project quality, language skills, extracurricular activities

HOW TO APPLY FOR DAAD:
- Apply online through DAAD portal: www.funding-guide.de
- Find scholarships at: www.funding-guide.de
- Select your scholarship programme then click "Submit application"
- Application only visible during active application period

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Academic qualifications and grades
- Quality of study/research project
- Language skills
- Motivation and future prospects
- Extracurricular commitment
- Special circumstances (disability, children, illness) considered

FAMILY BENEFITS:
- Partner allowance: 276 EUR per month
- Child allowance: 259 EUR per month per child
- Only for funding periods over 6 months

HEALTH INSURANCE:
- DAAD arranges private health insurance for scholarship holders
- Covers most pre-existing conditions
- Exceptions: HIV, multiple sclerosis, cancer, chronic kidney disease

WORK RIGHTS WITH DAAD SCHOLARSHIP:
- Allowed to work part-time in Germany
- Up to 140 days per year
