- AI in Society (M.Sc.) - 4 semesters, 120 credits

IMPORTANT NOTES FOR PAKISTANI STUDENTS:
- Most public universities in Germany are FREE (no tuition)
- Exception: Baden-Wurttemberg state charges 1,500 EUR per semester for non-EU students
- TU Munich charges 4,000-6,000 EUR per semester for Masters from winter 2024/2025
- All universities charge a semester fee of approximately 150-350 EUR
- Apply to multiple universities to increase chances
- Winter semester starts October, Summer semester starts April
- Most competitive programs require: IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 90+, strong Bachelor GPA

APPLICATION TIPS:
- Apply to at least 5-6 universities
- Include mix of top-tier and mid-tier universities
- Check each university's specific requirements
- Apply early - deadlines are usually 3-6 months before semester start
- Use uni-assist.de for centralized applications to many German universities
