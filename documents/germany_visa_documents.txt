GERMANY STUDENT VISA DOCUMENT CHECKLIST
Source: German Embassy Islamabad (Official) - Updated October 2023
For: Student Visa Section 16b Residence Act (Bachelor/Master/PhD)

VISA FEE:
- 75 EUR (paid in PKR)

MANDATORY DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
1. Application form - completed and signed, recent biometric passport photo glued on
2. Passport copies - copies of first two pages, passport validity: at least 1 year + 3 months
3. ID card copy
4. Application declarations
5. Appointment confirmation - printout from email confirmation
6. Written explanation for past visa refusals (if applicable)
7. Curriculum Vitae (CV)
8. Letter of admission from German university
9. Enrolment certificate (mandatory if semester already started, with letter from university confirming late join is allowed)
10. Confirmation of sufficient language skills in language of instruction
11. Proof of sufficient financial means - ONE of the following:
    - Blocked account (11,208 EUR for one year = 934 EUR per month)
    - Official obligation letter from Alien's authority in Germany (Verpflichtungserklärung)
    - Scholarship award letter
12. Proof of payment of tuition fees for first year (if applicable)
13. Documentation of academic and professional career:
    - Master students: previous bachelor degree certificate
    - Bachelor students: any previous academic degrees AND high school diplomas
    - All categories: previous employment certificates (if applicable)
14. Travel health insurance - valid from intended date of travel
15. Missing documents can be submitted to: missing.documents@isla.diplo.de with file number in subject line

FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Blocked account must contain 11,208 EUR for one year
- This equals approximately 934 EUR per month

LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS:
- Must confirm sufficient language skills in the language of instruction
- German programs: German language certificate required
- English programs: English language certificate required

VISA DURATION:
- Usually issued for up to one year
- Visa to seek university place: up to 9 months

HOW TO APPLY:
- Apply online through Consular Services Portal: digital.diplo.de/studium
- Embassy Islamabad: handles Islamabad Capital Territory, Gilgit-Baltistan, KPK, AJK, Punjab
- Consulate Karachi: handles Sindh and Baluchistan

WORK RIGHTS IN GERMANY:
- Can work up to 140 days per year with student residence permit
- Self-employed activities not permitted

PROCESSING NOTES:
- High volume currently - processing times longer than usual
- Incomplete applications cause delays
- Submit all mandatory documents immediately even if marked optional on portal
- Missing documents email: missing.documents@isla.diplo.de
